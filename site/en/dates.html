sTOUdio Dates

With sTOUdio DATES we want to promote meeting opportunities between current and former students and the business community. The main purpose is to stay in touch with each other, learn new things and look for opportunities for new collaborations. And where? On the field.

If reading the examples below gives you an idea of where students and mentors might “date” (online, at your home, at a youth centre, at the park, at a tourist provider near you, etc.), drop us a note and let the organizing begin!
